Our Story

Wall Street Discipline.
Bali Mastery.

Global Bali Home is a real estate investment & development holding. Three years on the ground in Bali, three active developments, 42 villas — from Rp 2.27B entry-level residences to Rp 6B+ luxury estates with private pools and projected annual returns of 10–15%.

Every project is designed, built, and managed under one roof — from architectural concept to rental income. We control each step to ensure quality, timeline, and return on your investment.
