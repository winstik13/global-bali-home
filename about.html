About Us

Our Story

Global Bali Home

Global Bali Home develops premium villa projects in Bali with projected annual returns of 12–15%. Three active projects, 42 villas — from $119K entry-level residences to $500K+ luxury estates with private pools.

Every project is designed, built, and managed under one roof — from architectural concept to rental income. We control each step to ensure quality, timeline, and return on your investment.

Founded by four international shareholders led by Mark Feldman, a Wall Street veteran with over $1 billion in transaction experience. We combine financial discipline with deep expertise in tropical architecture.

Mission

Our Mission & Vision

To create high-quality residential projects in one of the world's most dynamic property markets, delivering exceptional value for investors and residents alike.

We envision a future where sustainable development and luxury living coexist harmoniously, where every project enhances the natural beauty of Bali while providing world-class amenities and investment returns.

Sustainability

Our Sustainability Principles

We are committed to managing our environmental impact with care and contributing positively to the communities where we operate.

Every project incorporates sustainable building practices, locally sourced materials where possible, and designs that work with — not against — the natural landscape of Bali.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2023

Company Founded

Four international shareholders come together to establish Global Bali Home with a vision for premium real estate development in Bali.

2024

Serenity Villas Launched

Active land acquisition begins and our first project launches — 12 villa residences in a tropical jungle setting with natural waterfalls.

2025

Serenity Estates & Village

Two new projects launched — Serenity Estates, a boutique complex of 4 exclusive villas, and Serenity Village, a complex of 26 villas with shared amenities.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Mark Feldman

Founder & Managing Director

A seasoned investment banker with over 25 years of experience and $1B+ in Wall Street transactions. Mark's career spans structuring and executing complex financial deals across global markets.
