About Us

Our Story

Global Bali Home

Global Bali Home is an international real estate company focused on the development of high-quality properties in Bali — one of the world's most attractive property markets. Drawing on global expertise in architecture, construction, and investment, we create projects defined by strong design and long-term value.

Each development is delivered in line with international standards, combining solid investment potential with consistent, refined design. Our approach goes beyond aesthetics: we create spaces designed for both comfortable living and sustainable income.

Founded by four international shareholders with backgrounds in real estate development, finance, and investment, Global Bali Home brings together the character of Bali and international expertise through a strategic, forward-looking approach.

Mission

Our Mission & Vision

To create high-quality residential projects in one of the world's most dynamic property markets, delivering exceptional value for investors and residents alike.

We envision a future where sustainable development and luxury living coexist harmoniously, where every project enhances the natural beauty of Bali while providing world-class amenities and investment returns.

Sustainability

Our Sustainability Principles

We are committed to managing our environmental impact with care and contributing positively to the communities where we operate.

Every project incorporates sustainable building practices, locally sourced materials where possible, and designs that work with — not against — the natural landscape of Bali.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2023

Company Founded

Four international shareholders come together to establish Global Bali Home with a vision for premium real estate development in Ubud, Bali.

2024

Serenity Villas Launched

Active land acquisition begins and our first project launches — 12 villa residences in a tropical jungle setting with natural waterfalls.

2025

Serenity Estates & Village

Two new projects launched — Serenity Estates, a boutique complex of 4 exclusive villas, and Serenity Village, a community of 26 villas with shared amenities.

2026

New Projects Coming

Two new development projects in the pipeline. Details to be announced soon.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Mark Feldman

Founder & Managing Director

A seasoned investment banker with over 25 years of experience, Mark has spent much of his career on Wall Street, where he structured and executed over a billion dollars in transactions.

His deep understanding of finance, investment strategy, and real estate markets drives Global Bali Home's approach to creating developments that deliver both lifestyle value and strong returns for investors.

Under Mark's leadership, Global Bali Home has grown from a single project concept to a portfolio of three distinct developments in Ubud.
