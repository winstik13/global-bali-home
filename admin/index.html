Settings

What it does: Shows a popup when a desktop visitor moves the cursor toward closing the tab. Offers a free investment guide in exchange for an email — a lead capture tool.

Enabled: Turn the popup on or off across the entire site.

Trigger Delay: Minimum seconds a visitor must spend on the page before the popup can appear. Prevents annoying new visitors (recommended: 15–30 sec).

Countdown: After submitting the form, a countdown shows before the "Open Guide" button appears. Creates urgency (recommended: 5–10 sec).

Content: All texts are fully customizable per language. Badge, title, description, button labels, and success message.
