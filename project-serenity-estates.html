A New Standard of Living

Building on the original Serenity Villas success, this project elevates the concept to new heights of excellence. A boutique complex with only 4 villas — two 2-bedroom residences and two spacious 4.5-bedroom estates with staff quarters.

Each villa affords complete privacy and panoramic views, surrounded by tropical nature and rice terrace fields. Located just 8 minutes from Ubud's lively center.
